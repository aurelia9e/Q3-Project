Welcome!


Welcome to "Railway Navigator", a free-to-access MRT navigator that tracks the schedules, rates of stations for your convenience.


Established in 2019, Railway Navigator aims to make navigating the PMRT easier for those who are new to the railway system (or people who have never rode the MRT in their lives).
